-- WHAT IS MYTHIC PLUS? --

Spawned during Legion, Mythic Plus has become a staple within the WoW Community, with well over three million concurrent players. Mythic Plus takes a range of WoW Dungeons and puts the player up against a number of challenges; from enforced time limits through to additional affixes that a group must work together to overcome.
